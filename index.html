Brief Introduction

Fang Yuan is the ruthless, cunning protagonist of Reverend Insanity, a xianxia web novel set in a world of Gu Masters.

Core Traits
  • 500-year-old demonic cultivator reborn into his youth
  • Utterly pragmatic and emotionless
  • Driven by absolute freedom and personal gain

Known for his unmatched intellect and lack of moral restraints, Fang Yuan seeks immortality and power at any cost.

Philosophy
  • Survival of the fittest
  • True freedom beyond emotion, law, or fate
Legacy

Unlike typical heroes, Fang Yuan defies the traditional path of righteousness, making him one of the most complex and controversial anti-heroes in modern Chinese fantasy.
